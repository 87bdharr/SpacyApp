Named Entity Recognition (NER) is the process of taking raw text, and identifying importat features such as names, dates, locations. This has many use cases, for example, building sophisticated recommender engines for news websites based on your previously viewed content.

Python & SpaCy- How this app works

In essence, NER is teaching a program to how to read, and the fantastic SpaCy library is perfect for doing this. It's an advanced and open-source Natural Language Processing (NLP) library for Python. Using statistical models it builds from millions of lines worth of training data, SpaCy is able to extract meaning from text based on the content. For example, it's able to distinguish the word "Amazon" as being the company, rather than the forest, based on the way it's used in a sentence. This app supports the extraction of the following entity types:

  • PERSON: People, including fictional
  • ORG: Organisation, companies, agencies etc.
  • GPE: Geo-political Area, countrie, cities, states etc.
  • DATE: Absolute or relative dates or periods
  • MONEY: Monetary values, including unit
  • EVENT: Named hurricanes, battles, wars, sports events etc.
  • PRODUCT: Objects, vehices, foods etc.

This app will not extract every entity perfectly.


This is because it has been trained on previous examples, and if you use a word it doesn't recognise in that context, it will make its best effort to guess: but it may be wrong!
